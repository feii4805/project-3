Name - Kuroiso

Origin - Naruto (Chapter 1)

Date - September 1999

Kuroiso (黒磯) (26 years old), is Ai's bodyguard. He is a member of the Secret Service, Ai-chan's bodyguard and driver. He always wears a black business suit and sunglasses, akin to Secret Service agents. He has never been seen without his glasses but by the reaction of Ageo in one episode it can be assumed that he is quite handsome.He is very obedient to Ai-chan's orders because she blackmails him
